Hi,

I'm a first second year Ph.D. candidate at University of Notre Dame. My research interests include HCI, natural language processing, information visualization, and data mining. I'm using visualization and NLP techniques to help people explore data.
My advisor is Prof.Ronald A.Metoyer.I received my B.S. in Information Engineering from Beijing University of Posts and Telecommunications at 2015 Fall. I also worked as a Research Assistant at Multimedia Signal and Intelligent Information Processing Laboratory(MSIIP) with Prof.Ji Wu at Tsinghua University in my senior year.
